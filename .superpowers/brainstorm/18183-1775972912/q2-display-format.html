Question 2: How should the comparison results be displayed?

Once you pick two dates/weeks/periods, how do you want to see the difference?

A

Side-by-Side Table

Each measurement in a row, two columns showing the values, and a "Change" column with +/- and color coding.

Measurement     Apr 27     Apr 7     Change
Belly         95.0 cm    98.0 cm   -3.0 ▼
Chest         115.7 cm   114.0 cm  +1.7 ▲
L. Biceps     44.5 cm    43.0 cm   +1.5 ▲
B

Bar Chart (Before vs After)

Grouped bar chart with two bars per measurement — one for each date. Visual, great for spotting big differences at a glance.

Belly       Chest      Biceps
C

Change-Only Chart (Delta)

A single bar per measurement showing only the net change (+/-). Positive bars go up (green/red), negative go down. Instantly shows what improved vs got worse.

Belly   Chest   Biceps   Belly
D

All: Table + Charts together

Show the side-by-side table at the top with color-coded changes, plus a chart below (toggle between bar/delta views). Most complete view.
