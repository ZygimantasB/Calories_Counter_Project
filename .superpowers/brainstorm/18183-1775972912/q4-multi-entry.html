Question 4: If a week or period has multiple entries, which value to use?

For "Week vs Week" and "Period vs Period" modes, you might have logged measurements several times. Which one represents the week/period?

A

Most Recent entry in the range

Use the last measurement recorded within the week or period. Makes sense if you treat the latest as the "final" reading.

Week Apr 21–27: entries on Apr 21, Apr 24, Apr 27 → uses Apr 27
B

Average of all entries in the range

Average every measurement in the week or period. Smooths out daily variation.

Week Apr 21–27: entries on Apr 21 (95), Apr 24 (94), Apr 27 (93) → avg 94.0 cm
C

First vs Last within range (shows progress inside the range)

For a period comparison, show the first entry of each period vs the last — so you see how much you changed within each period, then compare those deltas.

Period A: first Apr 1 (98 cm) → last Apr 27 (93 cm) = -5 cm
Period B: first Mar 1 (102 cm) → last Mar 31 (98 cm) = -4 cm
D

Let me choose per comparison

A small toggle on the Compare tab to switch between "Latest" / "Average" / "First vs Last" when using week or period mode.
