Question 1: How do you want to select what to compare?

The Compare tab needs a way for you to pick which measurements to put side by side.

A

Two Specific Dates

Pick any two individual measurement dates and see them side by side. E.g., "April 27 vs April 7".

📅 Date A: April 27, 2025  ↔  📅 Date B: April 7, 2024
B

Week vs Week

Compare two calendar weeks using the average measurements recorded in each week.

🗓️ Week of Apr 21–27  ↔  🗓️ Week of Mar 31–Apr 6
C

Custom Period vs Custom Period

Define two date ranges and compare the first vs last measurement in each range. Good for "Month 1 vs Month 2" comparisons.

📆 Mar 1 – Mar 31  ↔  📆 Apr 1 – Apr 27
D

All of the Above

A mode switcher: tabs or a dropdown to switch between "Date vs Date", "Week vs Week", and "Period vs Period" comparisons.
